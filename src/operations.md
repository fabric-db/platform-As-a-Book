# Operations

Operations is where the platform proves itself.

The book must help an operator understand what to do when the system changes, fails, or drifts.

## Operational Views

The platform should expose:

- health
- readiness
- reconciliation status
- policy denials
- agent activity
- data movement
- cost
- incidents
- audit evidence

## Runbook Pattern

Each operational runbook should include:

- symptom
- impact
- likely causes
- checks
- recovery steps
- evidence to capture
- book updates required

## Continuous Learning

Operations is not separate from design.

Every incident should improve:

- the book
- the policy
- the schema
- the runtime
- the test suite
- the operator experience
